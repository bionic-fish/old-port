About Me


A self taught web developer and IT enthusiast. I began my interest in computers at an extremely young age and ever since has always been fascinated with technology. The pace in which engineering & development has taken the tech industry has been phenominal as both a hobbyist and avid learner of current web technologies.


As a hobbyist of PC building, a major research mindset comes into play when in search for the best items and upgrades at the best prices. Forever being on the look-out for new parts peaked my interest into how new and advanced technology can keeep producing updated models every other year and clearly display the benefits and accommodating features.


This began my path to functional programming. The beginning tutorial after the initial "Hello World!" introduction was a Tic-Tac-Toe game. Developing the basic concepts needed to complete this game using the javascript language with a bit of pre-build HTML & CSS. When I completed this personal challenge I felt estatic & excited that I had completed and build a basic interactive web based browser game.
